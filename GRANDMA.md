# 👵 GRANDMA.md

## What this project does (simple explanation)

This project is like a smart assistant for a college kitchen.

Every day, colleges cook food for students. Sometimes they cook too much and waste food. Sometimes they cook too little and students don’t get enough.

This system helps solve that problem.

It:
- Looks at student attendance
- Checks how much food is usually eaten
- Predicts how much food should be cooked tomorrow
- Suggests what food should be prepared each day

It also uses AI to:
- Create weekly food menus
- Give simple suggestions to reduce waste

## In short:
👉 It helps the college kitchen cook the right amount of food using smart predictions and AI.
